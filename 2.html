Janggu

Janggu is the Korean traditional musical instruments. I used the mixture of different brown colors to express the original color of Janggu and added details by using thin ropes.

The Hanji Flower

In order to express flower with a sense of antiquity, I used Hanji, a Korean traditional paper. Hanji appeared to be an effective choice as it well embodied the subtleties in the color of the flower.
